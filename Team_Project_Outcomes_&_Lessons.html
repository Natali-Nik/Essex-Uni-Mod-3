Team Project Outcomes & Lessons

Airbnb Business Analysis with Data Science: EDA, Market Insights, and Machine Learning Predictions for Real-World Applications.

Key Lessons and Outcomes

This group project reinforced my strengths in organization, project management, collaboration, research, writing, and strategic business thinking. I gained valuable exposure to exploratory data analysis (EDA) and predictive modeling using Python and its libraries, which were relatively new to me. The experience not only improved my technical skills but also provided a realistic work context and relevant insights into predicting profitable business cases.

Unit 6: Team Project - Assignment Details

Assignment Details: The group assignment involved analyzing Airbnb’s business using the AB_NYC_2019 dataset with a data science approach. The tasks included proposing a meaningful business question, performing data analysis with techniques like EDA, visualization, and clustering, and preparing a concise 1000-word analytical report with insights and recommendations for Airbnb's executive board.

Unit 6: Team Project - Key Outcomes and Lessons

  • Outcomes: The project "From Data to Decisions: Exploring Airbnb Market Dynamics in New York City", aimed to utilize the Airbnb NYC dataset and machine learning models to predict growth trends and average pricing for 2019. The methodology involved data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and predictive modeling, using Python and libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn. Key findings revealed that property types reflect neighborhood trends across NYC.
  • Recommendations: Recommendations included implementing dynamic pricing based on borough-specific trends, targeted marketing in growth areas like northern Brooklyn and Queens, regularly updating datasets, and enhancing private room features to attract solo travelers and couples.
  • Lessons Learnt: From this project, I learned the importance of clear communication and organization in team settings. Using a dedicated WhatsApp group for transparent decision-making worked well, ensuring everyone stayed informed and engaged. Having a project lead to manage time, agendas, and deadlines was crucial for maintaining focus, while assigning roles based on individual strengths in the first meeting streamlined the workflow and improved efficiency.
